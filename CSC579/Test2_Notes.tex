\subsection*{Stochastic Matrixes}
$P \in \mathbb{R}^{nxn} $ is \textit{stochastic} if :\\
 	\indent 1.) p_{ij} ~$\geq$ ~for~all  \textit{i} and \textit{j}.\\
 	\indent 2.) $\sum_{\mbox{all}~j} p_{ij} = 1}$ for all i.\\
 	\indent 3.) $\sum_{\mbox{all}~i} p_{ij} > 0}$ for all j.
 	
 \underline{Property 1} : Every stochastic matrix has and eigenvalue = 1
